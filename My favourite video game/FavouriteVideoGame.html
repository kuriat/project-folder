The Legend of Zelda:Breath of The Wild

The Legend of Zelda: BOTW is one of the multitude if games in the legend of Zelda franchise

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild[b] is a 2017 action-adventure game developed and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo Switch and Wii U. Set at the end of the Zelda timeline, the player controls an amnesiac Link as he sets out to save Princess Zelda and prevent Calamity Ganon from destroying the world. Players explore the open world of Hyrule while they collect items and complete objectives such as puzzles or side quests. Breath of the Wild's world is unstructured and encourages exploration and experimentation; the story can be completed in a nonlinear fashion
Wikipedia

The Back Story

The back story is: You are a knight chosen by the sword that seals darkness, the Master Sword, and you are commanded by the king to protect the princess. The king is told by a oracle that Calamity Ganon , a terrible evil, would rise. So the princess, Zelda, must unlock her special ability which is passed through all woman in the royal lineange. However, she cannot unlock those powers and so focuses on research. While researching they stumble upon four giant mechanical beasts, the divine beasts, that could be used to defeat Ganon, they also found lots of Guardians, robots that shoots a laser beam.

You and Zelda go and ask one people from the Zora(an aquatic fish-like race), Gorons(a large, muscular, mountain living race), Gerudo(a tribe of woman only who live in the desert) and the Rito(a bird-like species) to pilot each of the divine beasts. However, Calamity Ganon rises and takes control of the Divine Beasts and Guardians making them evil, the champions return and are killed by the mini-Ganons.
